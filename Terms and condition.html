Terms and Condition of our Barangay

Welcome to Barangay Mapagmahal! By engaging with our barangay, you agree to abide by the following terms and conditions:

1. Respectful Conduct: All residents and visitors must conduct themselves with respect and courtesy towards others at all times.

2. Compliance with Laws: All individuals within the barangay must comply with national, regional, and local laws, as well as barangay ordinances and regulations.

3. Participation in Community Affairs: Residents are encouraged to actively participate in barangay meetings, events, and initiatives aimed at improving our community.

4. Reporting Concerns: Residents are encouraged to report any concerns, issues, or incidents to the barangay officials promptly.

5. Prohibited Activities: Activities such as vandalism, theft, harassment, illegal drug use, and any other unlawful behavior are strictly prohibited within the barangay.

6. Responsible Pet Ownership: Pet owners must ensure that their pets are properly cared for, vaccinated, and restrained to prevent harm to others and maintain cleanliness within the barangay.

7. Environmental Responsibility: Residents are expected to properly dispose of waste, refrain from littering, and support efforts to protect and preserve the environment within the barangay.

8. Cooperation with Authorities: Residents are required to cooperate with barangay officials and law enforcement agencies in matters concerning public safety and security.

9. Resolution of Disputes: Any disputes or conflicts among residents should be resolved amicably and peacefully, with the assistance of barangay officials if necessary.

10. Updates and Notices: Residents are responsible for staying informed about barangay announcements, updates, and notices through official channels such as barangay meetings, bulletin boards, or official communication channels.

By residing or visiting [Barangay Name], you agree to adhere to these terms and conditions. Failure to comply may result in appropriate actions or sanctions by barangay authorities.

Thank you for being a responsible member of our community!
